Common Questions

Is it painful?
Not at all! You may feel a slight tingling sensation from the EMS microcurrents, which is completely normal and indicates the muscles are being stimulated. You can adjust the intensity to your comfort level.
How soon will I see results?
Most users notice an immediate "glow" and temporary lifting effect after the first 10-minute session. For long-term sculpting and wrinkle reduction, we recommend consistent use 3-4 times a week for 30 days.
Is it safe for sensitive skin?
Yes! The device uses medical-grade materials and non-invasive light therapy. If you have extremely sensitive skin, we recommend starting with the lowest intensity and using a gentle, water-based serum or gel.
Do I need to use a special gel?
While you can use it on clean skin, we highly recommend using a water-based serum, aloe vera gel, or your favorite moisturizer. This helps the device glide smoothly and improves the conductivity of the EMS microcurrents.
